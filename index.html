Question 01

Question 02

If a private virtual woman helped you, what would you want her to make you feel?

This tells us the emotional hook: confidence, companionship, attractiveness, calmness, or motivation.

Question 03

What would be most useful inside the app?

Pick the feature that would make you use it every week.

Question 04

If this genuinely helped you become more confident with women, would you pay?

This tests whether the idea is entertainment, self-improvement, or a real paid transformation product.
